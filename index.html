                    <h3>Funding & Scalability</h3>

                    <h4>Challenge:</h4>

                    <p>Building FightJudge.ai requires consistent funding for app development, server costs, and hardware R&D (smart glove integration).</p>

                    <h4>Our Plan:</h4>

                    <p>Allocate funds strategically across app development, AI, and marketing. Focus on MVP first — deliver fan value early, monetize later through subscriptions and partnerships. Maintain lean operations until product-market fit is achieved.</p>

                </div>

                

                <div class="risk-card">

                    <h3>Regulatory Adoption</h3>

                    <h4>Challenge:</h4>

                    <p>Athletic commissions and sanctioning bodies move slowly when adopting new judging technology.</p>

                    <h4>Our Plan:</h4>

                    <p>Start as a fan engagement tool (Phase 1) before targeting professional use (Phase 2). Build credibility through data integrity and proven results with fan scoring. Engage with commissions and promotions during development for feedback and alignment.</p>

                </div>

                

                <div class="risk-card">

                    <h3>Live Event Integration</h3>

                    <h4>Challenge:</h4>

                    <p>Synchronizing data in real time across global events and multiple sports (MMA and Boxing).</p>

                    <h4>Our Plan:</h4>

                    <p>Begin with regional and online events to refine infrastructure. Integrate delay-resistant data streaming and caching to ensure smooth scoring experiences. Develop promoter partnerships early to access official event feeds.</p>
